About Robert Couch

Robert Couch holds a Master of Architecture Degree from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, a Master of Architecture Degree from The University of Pennsylvania (Studio of Louis Kahn); was an exchange student at the Ulm School of Design, Ulm, Germany, has attended classes for a number of years at the Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts in Philadelphia.  Prices for all art work shown are available upon request. Robert Couch is a painter and architect living in Philadelphia.

His studio is located at:

Atelier Robert Couch
STUDIO 214 @ Scotts Mills
3510-30 Scotts Lane
Philadelphia, PA 19129
